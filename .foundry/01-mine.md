# Phase 1: MINE — Capture Raw Ideas

**Metaphor:** Mining ore from the earth. You don't know what's valuable yet — you just dig.

**Duration:** 30-60 minutes
**Mode applicability:** GREENFIELD only

---

## What Happens

The firehose. Brain dump everything. No filtering, no judging, no prioritizing. Every idea, concern, wish, frustration, dream — it goes in the pile.

### Inputs
- Client interview (recorded if possible)
- Existing materials (decks, emails, WhatsApp messages, screenshots)
- Competitor URLs
- "I wish it could..." statements
- Pain points ("I hate when...")

### Process

1. **Open the firehose** — Set a timer. 30-60 minutes. Capture everything the client/founder says. Use voice transcription if available.
2. **No editing** — Don't organize. Don't categorize. Don't judge. Just capture.
3. **Prompt the gaps** — After the dump, ask:
   - "What's the one thing that would make this worth building?"
   - "What's your biggest frustration with how it works today?"
   - "Who else has tried this? What did they get wrong?"
   - "If you had unlimited budget, what would you build?"
   - "What's the thing you haven't told me yet?"
4. **Capture medium** — Slack channel post, Google Doc, GitHub issue, voice memo. Doesn't matter. Just get it down.

### Outputs
- Raw idea dump (unstructured)
- Client quotes (verbatim — these are gold later)
- Initial problem statement (1-2 sentences, refined later)
- List of existing materials to ingest

### Kill Criteria (SHOWSTOPPERS)
- Client can't articulate the pain → STOP. No pain = no product.
- No buyer personas even vaguely defined → STOP. Who is this for?
- Technically infeasible at any budget → FLAG (don't kill, but flag loudly)

## ⚖️ R1: Scope Gate

See [ratify.md](ratify.md#r1-scope-gate-after-mine)

**Key question:** "Is this worth building? Is it scoped right?"

**Must pass:**
- [ ] Clear problem statement exists
- [ ] At least 1 buyer persona identified (even rough)
- [ ] 80-20 check: Can we get 80% of the value for 20% of the effort?
- [ ] No SHOWSTOPPERS triggered
- [ ] Confidence ≥ 8/10
