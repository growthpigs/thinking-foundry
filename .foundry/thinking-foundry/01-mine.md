# Phase 1: MINE — Raw Firehose of Ideas

**Duration:** 30-60 minutes
**Input:** User stories from Phase 0
**Output:** Raw unfiltered idea dump
**Prompt:** `poc/prompts/phase-1-mine.txt`

---

## What Happens

Brain dump everything. No filtering, no judging, no prioritizing.

The user says whatever's on their mind:
- Concerns
- Desires
- Constraints
- Half-formed ideas
- Worries
- Things that don't make sense yet

AI listens, asks clarifying questions, captures the firehose.

---

## Goal

Get everything out of the founder's head and into a structured capture. The goal is NOT to solve anything yet — just mine the raw ore.

---

## Transition

After 30-60 minutes of mining, move to **PHASE 2: SCOUT** (research possibilities)

---

## Ratify Gate (Before SCOUT)

Confidence check:
- Did we capture the core problem? (≥6/10?)
- Are the constraints clear?
- Do you feel heard?

If <6/10 confidence: stay in MINE, dig deeper.
If ≥6/10: proceed to SCOUT.
